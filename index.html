why choose BYU

Brigham Young University offers an exceptional education with renowned programs that challenge students academically while fostering personal and spiritual growth. Nestled in the breathtaking Provo Valley, BYU’s vibrant campus life is filled with engaging clubs, events, and traditions that create a strong sense of community. Whether you're cheering on nationally-ranked athletic teams like BYU football and basketball or participating in intramural sports, the energy and camaraderie on campus are unmatched. At BYU, you’ll gain more than just an education—you’ll create memories, friendships, and skills that last a lifetime!
